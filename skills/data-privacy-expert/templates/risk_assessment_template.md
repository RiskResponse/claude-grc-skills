## USAGE INSTRUCTIONS:

1. Complete this template before beginning high-risk processing
2. Involve DPO/Privacy Officer from the beginning
3. Consult with relevant stakeholders
4. Be thorough in risk identification
5. Document all mitigation measures
6. Update when processing changes materially
7. Review at least every 3 years
8. Maintain as evidence of compliance
9. Make available to supervisory authorities upon request
10. Use as living document, not one-time exercise
