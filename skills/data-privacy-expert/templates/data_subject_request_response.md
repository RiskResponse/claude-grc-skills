This document contains templates for responding to various types of data subject and consumer rights requests under GDPR and CCPA.

## INSTRUCTIONS FOR USE:

1. **Select appropriate template** based on request type and regulation
2. **Customize all [PLACEHOLDERS]** with specific information
3. **Check applicable boxes** (☐) where provided
4. **Remove inapplicable sections** or options
5. **Attach supporting documents** (data exports, certificates, etc.)
6. **Review for accuracy** - ensure all information is correct
7. **Obtain approval** from DPO or privacy team lead
8. **Send via appropriate method** (email, secure portal, certified mail)
9. **Document in tracking system** (use `scripts/dsr_tracker.py`)
10. **Maintain copy** for compliance records (3-7 years)

**Important Reminders:**
- Always verify identity before providing personal data
- Meet regulatory deadlines (30 days GDPR, 45 days CCPA)
- Document all requests and responses
- Train staff on using these templates
- Update templates when laws change
- Have legal counsel review customizations
